Recycling Resources

It can be hard to figure out what is recyclable, and we hope our website is a good start, but there are lots of additional resources online. Here are a few that we've found useful:


ecomaine has a comprehensive search tool that tells you what items are recyclable: https://www.ecomaine.org/recyclopedia/

berecycled.org offers recycling instructions by category: https://berecycled.org/how-to-recycle/

Be sure to check with your local recycling guidelines, as municipalities have different rules depending on what their facilities can process.
